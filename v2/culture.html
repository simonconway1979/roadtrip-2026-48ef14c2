Culture & Events

Two world-class piano recitals land on our dates — one in San Sebastián, one in Santander.

Heavy on the classical music Jo loves. Two festivals fall right on the itinerary, and between them they put two of the greatest living pianists on stage on our exact dates.

San Sebastián · 5–8 August

Quincena Musical (2–30 Aug)

One of Spain’s top classical festivals, running all month. - 6 Aug — Mahler Chamber Orchestra, Daniel Harding conducting, Daniil Trifonov (piano). Flying in lands us in town just in time — this is our San Sebastián night. 🎟️ book ahead - Daily chamber, early-music and organ side-concerts across the city.

Semana Grande / Aste Nagusia (8–15 Aug)

  • The International Fireworks Competition over La Concha runs nightly ~22:45, free — but it starts on the 8th, as we’re moving on to the mountains, so we’d catch the opening night at most. 🎆
  • If we’re still around on the 8th: giants-and-bigheads parades, traditional Basque music, trainera rowing regattas in the bay.

Cantabria coast · 11–14 August

Festival Internacional de Santander (3–31 Aug)

  • 11 Aug — Grigory Sokolov, solo piano recital (Sala Argenta). One of the greatest living pianists, the night before the eclipse, the evening we arrive on the coast. 🎟️ book ahead
  • 12 Aug — chamber concert in the church at San Vicente de la Barquera (the eclipse town). The eclipse comes first that evening.
  • 13 Aug — Dúo Elysium, country church at Arnuero.

🎟️ To book early

Two ticketed concerts will sell out: Trifonov (6 Aug) and Sokolov (11 Aug). Book once accommodation is confirmed — see the checklist. The Semana Grande street events and fireworks are free.
