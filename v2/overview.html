Overview

The whole trip at a glance — fly in, three bases, the eclipse, and where everything links.

Eleven days, three bases, almost no driving. We fly Barcelona → Bilbao, hire a car, and stay put in three places — the Basque coast, the green western Pyrenees, and the Cantabrian coast for the eclipse — then fly home from Bilbao. Flying instead of driving the long loop from Barcelona frees up about two whole days for the holiday.

Route: Fly BCN → Bilbao → San Sebastián → Ochagavía / Irati → Cantabria coast → Bilbao → fly BCN

Itinerary

Dates Stop Nights Travel in Headline
5 Aug Fly in, drive to San Sebastián — Fly ~1h20 + drive ~1h15 Land midday, settle in
5 – 8 Aug San Sebastián 3 — La Concha, pintxos, Trifonov
8 – 11 Aug Ochagavía / Irati 3 ~2h Beech forest, river, mountain calm
11 – 14 Aug Cantabria coast 3 ~3.5h Beaches, Picos, Sokolov, the eclipse
14 – 15 Aug Bilbao 1 ~1.5h Guggenheim, pintxos, easy exit
15 Aug Fly home from Bilbao — ~12 min + fly ~1h20 Relaxed last morning

10 nights total. One ~3.5h drive (Pyrenees → coast); everything else under two hours. The last night in Bilbao makes getaway day a 12-minute airport hop.
