Flights & Car Hire

We swapped the long drive from Barcelona for a short flight to the coast. It costs a bit more, but it removes about two full days of driving and gives the eclipse a four-night buffer.

Flights

Barcelona ⇄ Bilbao, Vueling, direct, ~1h20. Frequent through the day (early-morning to late-evening departures). Three return seats — 2 adults + Freya.

  • Out: 5 Aug, aim to land around midday so there’s time to collect the car and drive to San Sebastián.
  • Back: 15 Aug from Bilbao — book the afternoon so the last Bilbao morning (Guggenheim) is usable. We sleep in Bilbao the night before, so it’s a 12-minute hop to the airport.
  • Book: verify exact times and price before booking; an August return tends to run roughly €60–130 per person each way. Watch the cabin-bag vs hold-bag fare rules — with a car seat and beach kit we’ll likely want hold luggage.

Car hire

Pick up at Bilbao airport and drop back there (no one-way fee). Around 10 days.

  • Request a child seat for Freya (4) with the booking, or bring our own — confirm which when reserving.
  • A petrol/diesel car is simplest (an electric hire is pricier and less common, and we don’t need the range for short hops).
  • Compare the airport desks (Europcar, Hertz, Centauro, etc.) on total price including the seat, a second driver if wanted, and the excess/insurance.
  • A mid-size estate or small SUV gives boot room for three plus luggage.

The drives once we’re there

Short and easy — see Getting Around for the leg-by-leg:

Leg Time
Bilbao airport → San Sebastián ~1h15
San Sebastián → Ochagavía / Irati ~2h
Ochagavía → Cantabria coast ~3.5h (the one long one)
Comillas → Bilbao (last night) ~1h30
Bilbao → airport (getaway day) ~12 min

Documents & kit

  • Driving licences, EU cover via the hire, breakdown cover (usually included — check), EHIC/GHIC for the family.
  • The hire car carries the legal kit (vests, triangles / V16 beacon) — confirm at pickup.
  • Kid stuff — see with Freya.
