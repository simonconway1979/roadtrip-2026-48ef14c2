Flights & Car Hire

We swapped the long drive from Barcelona for a short flight to the coast. It costs a bit more, but it removes about two full days of driving and gives the eclipse a four-night buffer. Flights are booked; the car is chosen and books this evening.

Flights — booked ✈️

Barcelona ⇄ Bilbao, Vueling, direct ~1h15. Three seats (2 adults + Freya). Confirmation ZLUM3G · €1,000 total.

Date Flight Depart Arrive
Out Wed 5 Aug VY1422 BCN 09:40 BIO 10:55
Back Sat 15 Aug Vueling BIO 19:35 BCN 20:50
  • Morning out → land 10:55, car by ~11:30, in San Sebastián by ~1pm: a full first afternoon, no rushed start beyond an early alarm.
  • Evening back → the whole last Bilbao day stays usable; it’s a 12-minute hop from the Meliá to the airport.

Car hire — CUPRA Formentor (Sixt) 🚗

Picked the CUPRA Formentor — midsize SUV, automatic — from Bilbao airport, dropped back there (no one-way fee). €1,374.90 for the ~10 days. Booking this evening (needs the actual travel card present).

  • Pick up: Wed 5 Aug ~11:30 (after landing)
  • Drop off: Sat 15 Aug ~17:00 (before the evening flight)
  • Booking settings: “Best price” rate · 2,700 km included (plenty — our whole route is ~1,200 km) · child seat for Freya, or bring a packable booster · take standalone excess insurance rather than the desk’s upsell.

The options we compared

Three of us plus three suitcases needs real boot room, so the sweet spot is a midsize SUV / compact MPV — not a subcompact, not a van.

Car Type Boot Verdict
CUPRA Formentor Midsize SUV, auto 2 cases + 2 bags ✅ chosen — roomy, fun to drive, good price
BMW 2 Series Active Tourer Compact MPV 3 cases Boxiest boot + easiest to park
Volvo XC40 / BMW X2 Midsize SUV 2 + 2 Roomy alternatives
Seat Arona / Audi Q2 Compact SUV tight Fit 3 cases but no spare room
Seat Ibiza / Audi A1 / Citroën C3 Subcompact too small Avoid with 3 cases
