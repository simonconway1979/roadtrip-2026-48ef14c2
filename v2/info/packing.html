Packing

The range is the tricky part: hot Basque beaches, cool Pyrenean evenings, a smart night at the concerts, and a once-in-a-lifetime sky event — all flying out, so watch the cabin-bag rules.

Flying

  • Keep liquids within carry-on limits; decide hold vs cabin bags per the flight fare rules.
  • Freya’s car seat: bring our own (often free to check) or pre-book one with the hire car.
  • Sun cream and any liquids over the limit go in the hold or get bought on arrival.

Layers (mountain + coast)

  • Beach gear — swimsuits, towels, sun hats, high-factor sun cream.
  • Light layers and a fleece/light waterproof for cool Picos mountain evenings (and the cable-car top station).
  • A smarter outfit each for the Trifonov / Sokolov concerts.

Sun & heat

  • Strong sun cream, hats, everyday sunglasses.
  • Light, breathable clothes for the coast and valley days.

🌒 Eclipse kit

  • Certified eclipse glasses (ISO 12312-2) — several pairs, including one for Freya. Buy before the trip; they sell out.
  • Optional: a tripod / phone clamp for photos (don’t point a phone at the Sun without a filter during partial phases).

For Freya

  • Car entertainment, snacks, comfort items.
  • Beach toys, sun protection, a carrier/buggy for villages and forest walks.
  • See with Freya.
