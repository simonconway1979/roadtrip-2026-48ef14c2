Grand total

≈ €3,800–4,350 all-in for the trip. The fly-and-hire travel (~€1,000–1,350) is the swap that bought back two days of driving versus taking our own car.

Biggest single lever: San Sebastián accommodation. Staying just out (Hondarribia / Antiguo) with parking and driving in could shave a few hundred euros if you’d rather hold the total down.
