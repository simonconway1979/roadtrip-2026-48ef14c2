Packing

The range is the tricky part: cool Pyrenean evenings, hot beaches, a smart night at the concert, an inland heatwave in Zaragoza, and a once-in-a-lifetime sky event.

Layers (mountain + coast)

  • Light layers and a fleece/jacket for cool Aínsa evenings.
  • Beach gear — swimsuits, towels, sun hats, high-factor sun cream.
  • A smarter outfit each for the Quincena Musical / Sokolov concerts.

Sun & heat

  • Strong sun cream, hats, sunglasses (everyday).
  • Light, breathable clothes for Zaragoza’s heat.

🌒 Eclipse kit

  • Certified eclipse glasses (ISO 12312-2) — several pairs, including one for Freya. Buy before the trip; they sell out.
  • Optional: a tripod / phone clamp for photos (don’t point a phone at the Sun without a filter during partial phases).

For Freya

  • Car entertainment, snacks, comfort items.
  • Beach toys, sun protection, a carrier/buggy for town and valley walks.
  • See with Freya.

Car & docs

  • Driving docs, insurance, breakdown cover, EHIC/GHIC.
  • Reflective vests + warning triangles (legal requirement in Spain).
  • Chargers/cables (and EV cable if relevant — see driving).
