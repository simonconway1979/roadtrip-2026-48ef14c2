Grand total

≈ €4,350 all-in for the trip. Accommodation (~€2,620) sits inside the €3k budget; the rest is food, activities and travel costs.

Biggest single lever: San Sebastián. Staying just out (Hondarribia) with parking and driving in could shave ~€400 off accommodation if you’d rather hold the total down.
