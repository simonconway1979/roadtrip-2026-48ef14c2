The Drives

Seven legs, none over four hours — with stop ideas and charging per leg.

A deliberately gentle loop. Seven legs, nothing over four hours, built so each travel day ends somewhere worth arriving at.

Car is a BMW iX1 (electric) — full charging strategy on Driving & charging. Plan ~250–280 km between charges, loaded in August heat.

Barcelona → Aínsa · ~3h

Out of the city, up into the Pyrenean foothills. Climbs as you near Aínsa. - Stop idea: Barbastro (Somontano wine country) for lunch. - Charging: start full; this is over the comfortable single-hop range, so a short top-up around Lleida or Barbastro if needed.

Aínsa → Pamplona · ~2.5h

Across the Aragonese pre-Pyrenees into Navarre. - Stop idea: a riverside picnic; views of the Sierra de Guara. - Charging: single hop on a full battery — top up overnight in Aínsa if the base has AC.

Pamplona → Bilbao · ~1.5h

Short, easy motorway leg into Biscay. - Stop idea: quick one — push through. - Charging: easy single hop, no stop needed.

Bilbao → San Sebastián · ~1h

The shortest leg. Green coast and hills. - Stop idea: Getaria or Zarautz on the coast for a beach break en route. - Charging: easy, no stop needed.

San Sebastián → Cantabria coast · ~2h

West past Bilbao along the Bay of Biscay. - Stop idea: Castro Urdiales or a Bilbao pintxo if not done. - Charging: single hop; top up overnight in San Sebastián or on arrival in Cantabria.

Cantabria → Zaragoza · ~4h (the long one)

Inland and south, leaving the coast. The trip’s only 4-hour day — break it with a lunch stop. - Stop idea: Burgos (cathedral) makes a great mid-point break. - Charging: needs a charge — Burgos, timed with the lunch stop (charge 10–80% while you eat).

Zaragoza → Barcelona · ~3.5h

The easy run home down the AP-2. - Stop idea: Lleida if you need a leg-stretch. - Charging: one charge en route — around Lleida on the AP-2.
