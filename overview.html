Overview

The whole trip at a glance — route, itinerary, and where everything links.

A westward loop with the eclipse as the climax, then a short inland return. Longer stays, less driving — only six bases in sixteen days, and no single drive over four hours.

Route: Barcelona → Aínsa → Pamplona → Bilbao → San Sebastián → Cantabria coast → Zaragoza → Barcelona

Itinerary

Dates Stop Nights Drive in Headline
31 Jul – 3 Aug Aínsa 4 ~3h Ordesa, river swims, medieval village
4 Aug Pamplona 1 ~2.5h Old town, post-San Fermín calm
5 Aug Bilbao 1 ~1.5h The Guggenheim
6 – 9 Aug San Sebastián 4 ~1h La Concha, fireworks, Quincena Musical
10 – 13 Aug Cantabria coast 4 ~2h Beaches, Picos, the eclipse
14 Aug Zaragoza 1 ~4h Basílica del Pilar, last night
15 Aug Home → Barcelona — ~3.5h Easy final hop

15 nights total. Every drive ≤4h.
